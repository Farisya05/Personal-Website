My Education Journey

A reflection of my growth and learning experiences

Kindergarten Era

My kindergarten years at Tadika Alpha were a memorable and foundational period in my life. It was where I first learned to navigate the world of education while having fun. From morning songs to storytelling sessions, every activity felt like an adventure. I learned the basics of reading, writing, and counting through interactive games and engaging lessons. Art projects and outdoor play sparked my creativity, while class performances helped me build confidence and overcome shyness. The friendships I made and the encouragement from my teachers created a supportive environment. These early experiences fostered my curiosity and laid the foundation for my learning journey.

Primary School Era

Primary school was a time of learning, growth, and building the foundation for my future. From ages 7 to 9, I attended Sekolah Kebangsaan Subang Bestari, where I began developing essential skills and exploring my interests. After moving to a new home, I transferred to Sekolah Kebangsaan Seri Pristana, where I studied until I was 12. While my primary school experience wasn't always exciting, it taught me resilience and discipline. The dedicated teachers played a crucial role in shaping my academic journey, imparting valuable lessons that remain with me today. Looking back, I appreciate those formative years as stepping stones to who I am now.

High School Era

High school at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Saujana Utama was a transformative period in my life. I faced challenges that tested my resilience, but it was also a time of self-discovery. I explored new interests, joined clubs, and formed lasting friendships. The teachers and peers around me pushed me to aim higher and pursue my goals with determination. Although I encountered some setbacks, they taught me valuable lessons in perseverance and time management. Looking back, high school was a key foundation that shaped my academic growth and personal development, preparing me for the future ahead.

University Era

My university years at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) Kampus Rembau have been a pivotal chapter in my education. Beyond academics, this is where I met my true friends—people who have become my best friends. Together, we’ve navigated challenges and celebrated successes, building bonds that will last beyond university. These friendships have been a significant part of my journey, providing support and unforgettable memories. As I continue my studies and prepare for the future, I’m grateful for the connections I’ve made and the people who have been by my side.
