I have taken on several part-time jobs to gain experience and support myself financially. I used to work at Bakery shop named Chocolate Nine for five months while waiting for my first semestar to start in UiTM Rembau. Other than that, I also used to work as a kitchen helper at Academy Propandu's canteen during my second semestar break. My Experience Being a Cat Mother

Becoming a cat mother has been an incredible journey of love and responsibility. From feeding and grooming to creating a nurturing environment, I’ve learned patience and how to care for my feline family members. I have eight cats at home currently, three of them is a cute kitten. However, life is not always about joy. There's time when I have to face the affliction while being a cats' owner. After all, they have brought immense joy to my life!

My Internship Experience

During my internship at Muhibbin Group Sdn Bhd, I gained hands-on experience in corporate governance, assisting with statutory documents and ensuring regulatory compliance. I developed administrative, communication, and organizational skills while managing board meeting documentation and collaborating with stakeholders. Balancing multiple tasks under deadlines enhanced my time management abilities. This experience deepened my understanding of corporate structures, highlighted the importance of confidentiality, and inspired me to pursue a career contributing to organizational success.
